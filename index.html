Our Mission

At Gentle Threads, our mission is to provide people with Histamine Intolerance (HIA) clothing they are not only comfortable in, but also truly love to wear. We believe that living with sensitivity shouldn't mean compromising on style.

Every piece in our collection is carefully crafted with hypoallergenic materials and dyes to ensure your skin stays happy while you look fabulous.
